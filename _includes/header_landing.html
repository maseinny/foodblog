Taking you down to Chinatown, one bite at a time

Our little project to eat at all the places in Manhattan's Chinatown, where we live.

We have lived on the very edge of Manhattan's Chinatown since January 2014, which is almost the entire time we've lived in New York.

We eat in Chinatown more than anywhere else because of the proximity and the price. Not only are there an abundance of restaurants where you can eat a meal for well under $20 for 2 people, but the groceries in Chinatown are on average approximately 1/3 of the price as groceries anywhere else in Manhattan.

As an added benefit, Melissa is from Hong Kong, which means getting to speak Cantonese to everyone in the area makes her feel at home. We love this little gem of an area that we live in.
